Shah Foundation App Safety Standards

Child Safety and Protection Policy

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

The Shah Foundation app maintains a zero-tolerance policy regarding child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Any content or behavior related to CSAE is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate account termination and reporting to relevant authorities.

2. Content Monitoring

Our app implements the following safety measures:

  • All user submissions are reviewed before appearing on the leaderboard
  • Personal information collection is limited to essential data only
  • No direct messaging or user-to-user communication features
  • No image or media sharing capabilities

3. Reporting Mechanisms

Users can report concerns by:

  • Emailing: support@shahfoundation.com
  • Using the FAQ section in the app

4. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

We fully cooperate with law enforcement agencies in investigations related to CSAE and maintain records in accordance with legal requirements.

5. Regular Updates

These safety standards are regularly reviewed and updated to ensure maximum protection for all users.
